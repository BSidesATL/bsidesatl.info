Calling for Volunteers!

The BSides Atlanta Organizing Committee is seeking volunteers to help make the 2024 event possible. BSides Atlanta 2024 will be held on Saturday, September 14th 2024 at KSU.

The date for submission to apply is May 3rd. We will reach out after this date to interview will applicants by end of May.

* [Website and Social Media Coordinator](BSidesAtlantaWebsiteandSocialMediaCoordinator.pdf)
* [CFP Coordinator](CFPCoordinator.pdf)
* [Food and Beverages Coordinator](FoodandBevCoordinator.pdf)
* [Personal Sponsor Coordinator](PersonalSponsorCoordinator.pdf)
* [Speaker Coordinator](SpeakerCoordinator.pdf)
* [Villages Coordinator](VillagesCoordinator.pdf)
* [Volunteer Coordinator](VolunteerCoordinator_2024.pdf)

To apply, please complete this [Google Form](https://forms.gle/EHzjdRmFA5hqDNgy6) by May 3rd 2024. Please only apply for one role.
