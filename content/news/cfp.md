We welcome submissions for both technical and non-technical talks which address one or more areas of cybersecurity.

We are looking for 20-minute and 50-minute presentations. Please specify the length of your talk in your submission.
<!--more-->
**NOTE: All selected talks will need to be given IN PERSON. There will be no “stream-in” or “remote speaking” option made available under any circumstances.**

You can enter proposals until 2023-08-26 11:59 (US/Eastern), 3 weeks from now. Submit talks [here](https://pretalx.com/bsidesatl-2023/cfp).

We look forward to your submissions and to seeing you at BSides Atlanta 2023!
